About us

Founded in 2020 by alaa moatz, Donty was born out of a passion for baking and a commitment to quality. Using only the freshest ingredients and time-honored recipes, we create a variety of donuts and baked goods that cater to every taste, from classic glazed to exciting seasonal flavors.
Our mission is simple: to create the freshest, most delightful donuts that make your day a little brighter. We believe in the power of small moments of happiness, whether it's the first bite of a warm, glazed donut with your morning coffee or sharing a box of colorful treats with friends and family
Every donut we make starts with high-quality ingredients – sourced locally whenever possible – and a dedication to freshness. Our team of passionate bakers crafts each donut with care, ensuring every flavor is balanced and every texture is just right. We rise early each day, so you can enjoy a donut that’s made with love and baked to perfection.
